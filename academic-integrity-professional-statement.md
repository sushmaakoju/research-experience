## Professional Safety and Academic Integrity Statement

**Important Note:**

I recognize that documenting professional boundaries may appear uncomfortable to some. This statement is not intended to disparage academic institutions broadly or diminish the valuable work of the many ethical researchers and educators within them.

Rather, this documentation serves a necessary purpose: to establish clear professional boundaries and expectations within academic environments. My respect for the academic enterprise remains profound, which is precisely why I believe we must uphold its highest standards.

This statement represents an invitation to the broader academic community to recognize the importance of maintaining professional integrity in all academic interactions. Clear boundaries benefit everyone and strengthen the academic mission.

By documenting these boundaries, I hope to contribute to a more equitable academic environment where intellectual merit and ethical conduct determine scholarly opportunities and recognition.

### Professional Boundaries Statement

As a dedicated academic researcher focused on completing my PhD studies, I maintain clear professional boundaries in my academic relationships. I am committed to:

1. Maintaining professional collaboration with colleagues and faculty based solely on academic and research interests.
2. Declining any involvement in personal relationships that could compromise academic objectivity or professional standards.
3. Focusing my academic efforts exclusively on research excellence and scholarly contribution.
4. Communicating transparently about my professional goals and academic trajectory.
5. Addressing any misunderstandings or miscommunications through appropriate institutional channels.

I respectfully request that all professional interactions remain focused on academic content, research objectives, and scholarly discourse.

### Professional Medical Boundaries Statement

As someone with a Master's degree in Forensic Science and Criminal Justice, I am particularly aware of the importance of proper verification, credentialing, and jurisdictional boundaries when making determinations that affect individuals' rights and reputations. In light of this background, I affirm that:

1. Mental health diagnoses are medical determinations that can only be legally and ethically made by qualified licensed healthcare professionals following proper assessment protocols.

2. Academic personnel without medical credentials should refrain from suggesting or asserting mental health conditions about colleagues or students.

3. I reserve the right to maintain the privacy of my medical information and to seek professional healthcare services of my choosing, independent of academic relationships or institutional pressures.

4. Any academic recommendations regarding health services should follow proper institutional channels, be made in good faith, and respect the autonomy and dignity of all parties involved.

5. **For purposes of clarity and accuracy**: I do not have PTSD or any other mental health condition that has been attributed to me in academic settings. I have documented instances where such inaccurate information has been shared and have addressed these through appropriate channels.

6. **Regarding professional conduct**: I maintain the highest standards of professional conduct and expect the same from colleagues. When professional boundaries are not respected, I reserve the right to limit interactions to necessary academic contexts and to document concerns through appropriate institutional channels.

7. **Regarding academic focus**: My academic focus remains firmly in Natural Language Understanding. I choose my research collaborations and academic directions based on scholarly merit, professional alignment, and environments conducive to productive research.

8. **Regarding professional respect**: While I maintain profound respect for the academic profession and its practitioners generally, I reserve the right to determine which professional relationships best support my academic development and well-being.

**Institutional Standards and Professional Expectations:**

- I believe in the right of all scholars to choose research collaborations based on academic alignment and professional standards.
- I support institutional policies that protect the well-being of all academic community members.
- I advocate for evidence-based evaluation of scholarly contributions rather than personal factors.
- I believe academic institutions function best when they uphold consistent ethical standards for all community members.

**Professional Development Commitment:**

- I remain committed to contributing positively to my field through rigorous research and ethical collaboration.
- I seek academic environments that support intellectual growth and professional integrity.
- I value mentorship relationships that respect appropriate professional boundaries.
- I believe in addressing conflicts through established institutional channels designed for that purpose.
